THE STORY


I started this business when I was 17 years old. At first, it began as a hobby, I was selling jewelry to my friends but gradually clientele grew and Moonvy actually became a small business. Now I am based in Denmark although I deliver my ideas and products to the whole world.


Moonvy Jewellery creates good-quality, original, adorable, K-POP related and affordable necklaces, earrings, jewelery sets for women that fit any occasion. Every piece is special and made with a lot of love, therefore, it brings an unforgettable experience to everyone who is wearing it.




DAY AS A JEWELLERY DESIGNER


Since I am still a student, my workday usually starts in the afternoon. Most of the days, I sketch my ideas on the tablet. Later, I experiment with different materials and designs for the future pieces. Wednesdays and Sundays are the days when I deal with the orders and ship them to my customers.




MATERIALS I USE


I have a manufacturer in China, therefore, I usually send them my designs and the list of materials I need and they see if they are able to get it for me. When I see a pedant I like in a shop, I buy it and test it with my designs. If it works, I buy more of it. All of the items are carefully packed and sealed in a plastic bag which is then put into an organza bag and sent to the customer in a bubble envelope.
