MOONVY Policy


Please make sure to read before placing your order! Once you proceed to check-out and place your order, you accept the following terms and conditions.


Data and Personal information

When placing an order, information regarding your shipping address, bank information, personal data and other truthfully contact information will be kept under us. You agree that the informations will be kept, and that we may use it to contact you. MOONVY does not share your personal data and information with anyone. When using our service, you provide us with information that will only be used in regards of your order. We respect your privacy!


    Data we collect and save:

  • Name, email address, postal code
  • Contact information provided by you
  • Demographic information
  • Payment information (such as a credit card)

Payment

We accept PayPal and different types of credit cards. Cash is not accepted unless anything else has been stated by MOONVY. All items are exclusive to the shipping fee and taxes. This means that the shipping will not be applied until check out. The total cost of the shipping variates from the customer’s chosen shipping method.


Prices may change at any time, but changes shall not affect the orders for which we have sent an order confirmation out to and there will be no additional charges other than what has been stated at the check out.


Shipping and Returns

We ship with PostNord, which is a local Danish shipping company. For customers who do not want their order to be shipped, due the fact that this is an online business, there will be no possibilities of picking up your order at our office. Depending on your choice of shipping method, tracking is available.


Once the order has been shipped, MOONVY does not have any responsibilities anymore, unless the customer has chosen the tracked shipping method. We do not offer any refunds for lost items, and can anytime refuse to offer help. It is solely up to the buyer to decide what shipping method they want and the benefits it comes with.


Due to the current COVID-19 pandemic, please be aware that there can be a delay to the estimated delivery time. For orders outside Denmark, it might take up to 10 days before your order arrives. We kindly ask you to be patient.


Returns

All MOONVY’s products are eligible for returns, but the return shipping is solely on the customer. We do not take returns for damaged products caused by the customer or if the packaging has been ruined or opened. Once MOONVY receives the item, we will contact the buyer and settle refund of the product. We do not hold responsibility for lost products in the mail, and can any time refuse to accept the return if the product quality has been damaged.


It is important to send a notice to MOONVY before returning the product.


Refunds

MOONVY does not offer any refunds on sale products, customized products or products bought through deals and offers.


Cancellations of orders can be done up to 24 hours after purchase. Once the deadline has passed and the order has been shipped, a refund can not be done unless the buyer sends back the items in its original packaging and condition.


    Circumstances in which you’re eligible for refund or re-ship products:

  • Errors in the earring (such as discoloration, unequal size, missing part etc)
  • Damage during the shipment process
  • Not the same product you ordered
  • Jewelry was already damaged upon delivery

Please notice that MOONVY will only refund the price of the product, we do not refund the shipping as once it has been created, the price has been paid. When placing an order, you accept that the shipping rates will not be refunded!


Damaged products

If the product has any errors, please contact MOONVY for a repair or re-ship. We take responsibilities for damaged products to some extends. If the product has been damaged during the shipping process, we will gladly repair it or send a new one. This only counts if the damage has been proven to be a manufacturing error or due to the shipment.


If you wish to return the damaged product or send it back for repair, the shipping fees and repair fees will be on you.


Warranty

There is no warranty on our products. However, we do offer free repairs if your jewelry fell apart due to unsoldered jump rings or loose ends. If your repair requires a replacement of a finding such as pendant, hoops, chains, ear cuffs etc, there might be an extra charge.


For any questions regarding our Policy and Terms and Conditions, please contact MOONVY.
